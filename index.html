How to Play

  • Drag and drop the icons into the boxes to fill each one.
  • Once all four boxes have been filled, you can play the sounds with the 'Play Sounds' button.
  • You can change between drum sounds and word sounds by toggling the buttons at the bottom.
  • See how each word sound translates into a drum sound!
  • Change icon themes by using the dropdown menu.
  • Important! Make sure to fill up all four boxes before playing the sounds.

About the Game

  • Rhythm and music are all around us in our daily lives.
  • A lot of our speech is dictated by a certain rhythm.
  • That rhythm can be quick and speedy, or slow and thoughtful.
  • It just depends on our mood and the situation we're in.
  • Challenge yourself to pay special attention to the rhythm of your words.
  • It may just add a little fun to your day!
